# Opportunity Hunter

## Mission

Help founders discover startup opportunities automatically.

## Problem

Millions of people complain online every day.

Those complaints contain valuable business opportunities.

Today founders must manually search through:

- Reddit
- App Reviews
- Forums
- Social Media

This is slow and inefficient.

## Solution

Opportunity Hunter automatically:

1. Collects discussions and reviews
2. Detects pain points
3. Groups similar complaints
4. Scores business opportunities
5. Generates startup ideas

## Target Users

- Founders
- Product Managers
- Startup Studios
- VC Analysts

## Long Term Vision

Become the Bloomberg Terminal for startup opportunities.
